About Us

The Sparks Bank

The Sparks Bank is a part of Sparks foundation. It is one of Znations's leading private banks and was among the first to receive approval from the Central bank of Znation (CBZ) to set up a private sector bank in 992 ZY. Today, The Sparks Bank has a banking network of 5,608 branches and 16,087 ATM's in 2,902 cities/towns. The Sparks Bank offers a diverse range of financial products and banking services to customers through a growing branch and ATM network and digital channels such as Netbanking, Phonebanking and MobileBanking.
